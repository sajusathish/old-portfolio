Kuwait children's hospital

Unique to Kuwait's sea faring heritage and proximity to the sea, the Sea Shanty narrative describes an experiential environment based on the story of the great sea adventure. The depths of the sea are described in a colour gradient simulating ascension from the trenches of the sea floor to the surface. Marine life occupies the waterscape describing the regional ecology. Fictitiously scaled creatures describe the wondrous perspective of childhood.
