Ananda Roha luxury Resort & Spa

Ananda in the Sahyadris is a destination resort and spa tucked away within the pristine isolation of a UNESCO conservation area. This luxury resort and its branded residences are sprinkled across the shores of five private lakes in the Western Ghats mountain range of Maharashtra, India. The architecture and design of Ananda aspire to create a seamless transition between the natural beauty of the exteriors and the elegance of the interior spaces.
